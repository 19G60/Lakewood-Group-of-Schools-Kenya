Facilities

At Lakewood Schools, we believe that a strong learning environment goes hand in hand with excellent facilities. Our modern classrooms, well-equipped laboratories, libraries, and ICT hubs foster curiosity and innovation. Spacious playgrounds, sports grounds, and creative art spaces ensure that learners grow holistically—mentally, physically, and socially. Every facility is designed to provide comfort, safety, and inspiration, creating an environment where students can thrive.

Programmes

Lakewood Schools offers a wide range of programmes designed to nurture every learner’s potential. From foundational learning in early years to advanced levels in primary and secondary, we integrate both the Competency-Based Curriculum (CBC) and global best practices. Our programmes are enriched with co-curricular activities—sports, music, drama, robotics, environmental clubs, and entrepreneurship—that empower learners with essential life skills. Each programme is crafted to support academic excellence, character formation, and holistic growth

Extracurricular Activities

At Lakewood Schools, we believe that education goes beyond the classroom. Our extracurricular programmes give learners opportunities to discover and nurture their unique talents, build confidence, and develop teamwork. From drama, music, and debate to robotics, sports, environmental clubs, and journalism, our learners engage in enriching activities that complement their academics. These experiences empower students to apply knowledge in real-life situations, explore their passions, and grow into well-rounded individuals.

Events

Lakewood Schools is a vibrant community where learning is celebrated beyond the classroom. Throughout the year, we host events that bring together learners, parents, and the wider community. From graduation ceremonies, talent shows, and cultural days to sports tournaments, academic exhibitions, and career fairs, every event is designed to inspire growth, showcase talent, and strengthen bonds. Our events not only celebrate achievement but also provide learners with platforms to explore, innovate, and shine.
